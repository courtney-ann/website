About Us


Driver drowsiness is a common cause of vehicular accidents. The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA)

reports that every year, there are an estimated 100,000 crashes related to drowsy-driving that are reported to the police

in the United States alone. Despite this prevalence of driver fatigue, many vehicles lack driver drowsiness detection systems.

This website is intended to record instances when drivers are drowsy or become involved in an accident as a result of fatigue.

This information is obtained using an embedded device with a deep-learning model trained to classify drivers as either drowsy

or awake.
